OVERVIEW
The Rosamond Gifford Zoo is home to more than 700 animals of all shapes and sizes. From the smallest animal (milkweed bug) to the largest (Asian elephant), the zoo offers unique viewing opportunities for its guests!
NEW ARRIVALS
Come check out the zoo's newest arrivals! The Rosamond Gifford Zoo's 40th Humboldt penguin chick hatched on January 9, 2015 to Mario and Montana and weighed 79 grams. We are also pleased to announce the addition of the longsnout seahorse to our aquatic collection. The group, which can be seen on exhibit, is in the aquarium located in the zoo's Adaptation of Animals area, across from the sloths.
MAMMALS
The Rosamond Gifford Zoo is home to a large host of mammals, ranging from the African Lion to the Guinea Hog to the Spectacled Bear. Come visit to see these beautiful animals for yourself!
BIRDS
Our diverse collection of birds is also sure to amaze. Come see our Red-Tailed Hawk, Lavendar Waxbill, Western Bluebill, and dozens of others. The Diversity of Birds Aviary will have everything you are looking for!
AMPHIBIANS &
REPTILES
Come to the U.S.S. Antiquities to see some of our amphibians and reptiles. Our spotted turtle is waiting for you! Come take a look at this little guy and our many other amphibians and reptiles. If snakes are your thing, come see our Green Tree Python. If you like lizards, the Blue Spiny Lizard is here!
FISH
Our diverse collection of birds is also sure to amaze. Come see our Red-Tailed Hawk, Lavendar Waxbill, Western Bluebill, and dozens of others. The Diversity of Birds Aviary will have everything you are looking for!
INVERTEBRATES
Invertebrate's are animals that do not have a backbone (like a spine in humans). Believe it or not, coral is an animal! Come take a look in the U.S.S. Antiquities to see our collection of invertebrates, which includes starfish, shrimp, Halloween Crabs, and many others!
